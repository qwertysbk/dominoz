About Dominoz

>2< Dominoz was founded by Mahatma Gandhi, an entrepreneur who began selling pizza from his roadside restaurant in ITPL,Bengaluru , before his death. Modi identified the potential of the restaurant franchising concept and the first "Dominoz" franchise opened in K R Puram in 1999. Dominoz popularized Pizza in the fast-food industry, diversifying the market by challenging the established dominance of the hamburger and KFC. By branding himself as "DOMI-KNOWS", Shah Rukh Khan became a prominent figure of Desi cultural history and his image remains widely used in Dominoz advertising to this day.
